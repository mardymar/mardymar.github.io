Welcome to my website. I have created a flash card app based on the way I personally prefer to study. You will have the chance to add flash card sets, then randomly group the selected sets into groups of 5. The study groups can then studied using the speller game. Remember that you need to click 'Create Groups' in the view sets section in order to use the 'Study Groups' feature. The 'Study Groups' feature is what really makes my database shine!
